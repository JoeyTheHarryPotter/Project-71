What's New

  • Virtual Learning

    Due to COVID-19, we are now doing learning virtually. Students will have to follow a certain schedule throughout the day. Teachers will help the students if nessecary and give directions for the students to follow.

  • Attendence

    Attendence will now be taken on a google form as a result of COVID-19. Student will be required to fill it out daily. Teachers will be required to check the attendence and make sure everyone is present (unless they are sick).
